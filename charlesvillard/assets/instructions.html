--- Fonts Assets ---
The Google fonts Open Sans and Roboto Slab are the fonts used primarily throughout the site. 
To include the Google Font, put the following in your HEAD tag:

<link href='https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Open+Sans:800|Roboto+Slab:400,700' 
      rel='stylesheet' type='text/css'>

You will of course, need to declare the font-family property in your CSS.
